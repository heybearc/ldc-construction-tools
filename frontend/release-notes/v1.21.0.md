Stay informed about your feedback with our new comprehensive notification system!

**What you can do:**

- Dismissible banner alerts with bell icon
- Real-time screenshot previews in feedback forms
- Direct links to view your feedback

### üìÖ Project Schedule Change Notifications

Schedule managers can now notify team members when project schedules are updated!

**What you can do:**
- Send email notifications when schedules change
- Choose which roles to notify (coordinators, crew members, etc.)
- Include a description of what changed
- See how many people will be notified before sending

**How to use it:**
1. Update your project schedule as usual
2. After saving, you'll see the Schedule Change Notification modal
3. Describe what changed in the schedule
4. Check "Send email notifications" if you want to notify people
5. Select which roles should be notified
6. Click "Create & Notify" to send

**What recipients receive:**
- Email with project name and schedule changes
- Description of what changed and why
- Schedule version information
- Link to view the updated schedule
- Their role on the project

- Feedback notifications are sent automatically
- Check your spam folder if you don't receive emails
- You won't receive notifications for your own comments
