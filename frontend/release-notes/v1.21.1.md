### ğŸ’¬ Add Screenshots to Comments

Admins can now **paste screenshots directly into comments** when responding to feedback!

**How it works:**
1. Open any feedback item in the admin panel
2. Click "Add Comment"
3. Type your comment
4. Press **Ctrl+V** (or **Cmd+V** on Mac) to paste a screenshot
5. See a live preview of your screenshot
6. Remove unwanted screenshots with the X button
7. Submit your comment with screenshots attached

**What users see:**
- Comment text with screenshots displayed inline
- Screenshots shown in a 2-column grid below the comment
- Click screenshots to view full-size
- Clear visual communication between admins and users
