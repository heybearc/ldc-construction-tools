### üìã What's New

**Multiple Role Assignments**
Volunteers can now be assigned multiple organizational roles, making it easy to track regional coordinators, project staff, and Construction Group leadership positions all in one place.

**What you can do:**
- Assign volunteers to regional roles (Field Reps, Design Contacts, Housing Contacts, etc.)
- Track project-specific assignments (Project Staffing Contacts, Safety Coordinators, etc.)
- Identify Construction Group leadership (CGO, ACGO, CG Support)
- Mark primary vs. secondary roles
- Link roles to specific teams, crews, or projects

**How to use it:**
1. Go to the **Volunteers** page
2. Click the **Edit** button (pencil icon) on any volunteer
3. Scroll to the **"Organizational Roles"** section
4. Click **"Add Role"** to assign a new role
5. Select the role category and specific role
6. Optionally link to a team, crew, or project
7. Mark as primary role if it's their main responsibility
