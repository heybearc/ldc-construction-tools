### Personnel Team Permissions Now Working

Fixed an issue where organizational roles (like Personnel Contact Assistant, Personnel Contact, etc.) were not being properly recognized by the system.

**What was fixed:**
- Users with Personnel Team roles now see all management buttons they should have access to
- "Submit on behalf of" functionality now works correctly for Personnel Team members
- All organizational roles are now properly detected and applied

**Who this affects:**
- Personnel Contact (PC)
- Personnel Contact Assistant (PCA)
- Personnel Contact Support
- Other organizational role holders

**What you need to do:**
- If you have an organizational role, you may need to log out and log back in to see the updated permissions
- After logging back in, you'll see the management buttons (Add, Edit, Delete, Import, Export, etc.) that your role grants access to
