### Fixed: Personnel Can Now Assign Crew Requests

**What was broken:**
Personnel Contact users couldn't assign crew requests to team members - the dropdown was empty.

**How it's fixed:**
The system now properly recognizes Personnel Contact roles (PCA, PC) and shows all personnel users in the assignment dropdown.

**What you'll see:**
- "Assign to..." dropdown now shows all personnel team members
- Works for both primary and secondary organizational roles
- Easier workload distribution among your team
